# CharacterForge UI Asset Prompts

## 1. Character Sheet Background / Header
**Prompt:** A high-resolution, cinematic wide-angle background for a Dungeons and Dragons character sheet. Dark, moody atmosphere with subtle parchment textures, embers floating in the air, and a hint of a medieval armory or library in the soft-focus background. Deep blacks and reds. 4k, digital art style.

## 2. Fighter Class Icon
**Prompt:** A stylized, minimalist icon of a crossed longsword and shield, glowing with a faint silver light against a dark circular stone background. High contrast, sharp edges, fantasy RPG UI style.

## 3. Attribute Icons (Strength, Dexterity, etc.)
**Prompt:** A set of 6 matching fantasy icons for RPG attributes: a muscular arm (Strength), a winged boot (Dexterity), a shield (Constitution), an open book (Intelligence), an eye (Wisdom), and a theatrical mask (Charisma). Stone-carved texture, glowing runes, dark background.
